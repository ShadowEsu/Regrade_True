## Full description

Regrade helps students make sense of already-graded work. Upload a marked exam or import it from an approved source, review scores, rubric items and teacher feedback, and turn recurring mistakes into a focused review plan. When the evidence supports a question, Regrade can help draft a respectful appeal for you to review.

Mr Whale, Regrade’s AI coach, can explain marked questions and help with math, chemistry and written work. AI suggestions may be incomplete or wrong and do not prove a grading error. Regrade does not change grades or send an appeal without your approval.

Available features depend on your plan, institution, connector authorization and region. Institution-controlled integrations are not available until approved and verified. Manual PDF and image upload remains available.
