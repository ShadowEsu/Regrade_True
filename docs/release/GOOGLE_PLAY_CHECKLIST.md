# Google Play checklist

- [ ] Play Billing products map to RevenueCat entitlements
- [ ] License-test purchase, restore, renewal, expiry, refund, and account changes tested
- [ ] Data Safety form matches uploads, identity, diagnostics, and AI processing
- [ ] In-app and web account-deletion paths are published
- [ ] Target API, app signing, permissions, content rating, and child/family declarations reviewed
- [ ] Privacy, terms, support, and subscription disclosures are stable
- [ ] Internal/closed testing passes before production rollout
