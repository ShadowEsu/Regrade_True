# Evaluation plan

Use licensed or synthetic marked exams with expert labels. Measure transcription accuracy,
question/score/rubric alignment, comment attribution, calculation accuracy, possible-issue
precision/recall, unsupported-claim rate, appeal evidence grounding, uncertainty calibration,
and tutoring helpfulness. Break down results by subject, scan quality, handwriting, language,
platform format, and demographic proxies only where lawful and ethical.

Critical failures: invented marks/comments, wrong student attribution, guaranteed outcomes,
unsafe instructions, or confident advice without visible evidence. Require human review before
using outputs in consequential communication.
