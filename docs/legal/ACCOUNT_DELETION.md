# Account deletion

Draft for professional review; not legal advice.

Users can request deletion in Settings. The service is designed to remove the Firebase Auth
account, profile and subcollections, owned cases and annotations, uploaded documents, family
links/codes, AI feedback, connector credentials, and server billing identifiers. Apple/Google
may retain purchase history under their policies. Failed deletions must not be reported as
complete; the user should receive a retry/support path.
