# Parent and Guardian Consent — Draft Requirements

**Not legal advice. Not an approved consent form. Counsel must review this before publication or collection of child data.**

A production consent flow should clearly identify Regrade, the child/learner, the consenting adult, data categories, purposes, AI processing, school-platform access, retention, third parties, revocation, deletion, support contact, and the consequences of declining consent.

The consent record should store the policy/version accepted, adult identity verification method, learner relationship, region, timestamp, permission scope, and revocation history. It must not rely only on a checkbox or possession of a pairing code where verifiable consent is legally required.

Current pairing approval is a product authorization control, not a complete legal parental-consent system.
