# AI disclosure

Draft for professional review; not legal advice.

Regrade uses AI to read submitted educational material, explain feedback, identify possible
grading inconsistencies, suggest study priorities, and prepare appeal drafts. AI can misread
handwriting, equations, rubrics, scores, and context. Outputs are not official grade decisions
or legal advice and do not guarantee recovered marks. Users must verify source material and
approve any communication before it is sent.
