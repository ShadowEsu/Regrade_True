# Teacher and school use terms

Teacher or school use requires authorized accounts, least-privilege learner access, appropriate institutional and family permissions, accurate role information, and compliance with applicable school policy. A pairing code alone does not establish legal authority.

Regrade must not be used to impersonate a learner, submit an appeal without confirmation, access records outside the granted scope, or treat AI findings as official grading decisions. Institutional contracts, data-processing roles, retention, support, and incident duties require separate approval.
