# Australian school pilot plan

## Proposed scope

One institution, one subject/cohort, 10–30 consenting participants, four weeks, manual import first. Connector activation is a separate gate. No grade write, full roster, parent-contact, attendance, welfare, medical or finance data. No automatic appeal sending.

## Approval owners

Identify whether authority sits with the school, governing company, state/territory department, Catholic diocese/system, TAFE/university IT or platform vendor. Obtain written ICT, privacy and educational sponsor approval; parent/guardian consent where required.

## Australia-specific gates

- Privacy impact assessment and applicable APP/state/territory/sector review
- Data location and overseas-disclosure terms, including AI processing
- Child capacity/consent and supervisor pairing rules
- Notifiable Data Breaches/incident contact and contract deadline
- Records retention/deletion, accessibility and support
- Named human review of AI suggestions and prohibited teacher-error/mark-recovery claims

## Acceptance and stop conditions

Use the global `PILOT_IMPLEMENTATION_PLAN.md`. Stop for cross-account data, unapproved overseas transfer/scope, automatic external sending, exposed credentials, or inability to delete/revoke. Report comprehension and workflow quality—not recovered marks—as the primary outcome.
