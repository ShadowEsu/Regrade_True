FAQ

Quick answers

Does Regrade email my professor for me?

No. Regrade only helps you draft. You copy, edit, and send from your own email when you’re ready.

What should I upload?

Your rubric, graded assignment, and any professor or TA feedback. A clear photo or PDF from Canvas works great.

Will my email sound rude?

That’s what the tone check is for. Regrade nudges your draft toward respectful, professor-safe language.

Is this only for big grade changes?

Not at all. Sometimes you just need clarity — or a second look at one rubric line. That’s a valid ask.
